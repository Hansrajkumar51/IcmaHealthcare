About Us

Indian Medical Coding Academy (IMCA)

Indian Medical Coding Academy (IMCA)

Founded in 2020, IMCA provides industry-standard medical coding education, practical revenue cycle management training and staffing services. We focus on hands-on skills, certification readiness and measurable placement outcomes for our students.

  • Certified trainers (AAPC & AHIMA)
  • Practical case-based learning
  • Dedicated placement support

Leadership

Meet the visionaries leading IMCA Healthcare with excellence and integrity

Surjeet Singh

Founder & CEO

Surjeet Singh is the Founder and driving force behind IMCA Healthcare.
