About Us

Indian Medical Coding Academy (IMCA)

Who We Are

Indian Medical Coding Academy (IMCA) is one of the leading Medical Coding firms established in 2020, dedicated to providing Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) services, Healthcare Training & Staffing from coder to manager level.

  • International standard training & placement
  • AAPC & AHIMA certified trainers
  • Expertise in ICD-10 CM, ACS, ACHI
  • End-to-end RCM & staffing services

What We Do

Delivering quality training, staffing, and healthcare solutions

Training

100% practical training in Anatomy, ICD-10 CM, HIPAA compliance, specialty coding, and certification courses (CPC, CRC, CCS).

Placement Support

Boot camps, interview prep, campus drives, and workshops to ensure 100% placement support for trainees & interns.

Staffing Solutions

Providing trained coders and managers for healthcare organizations through innovative workforce solutions.

Our Mission

To provide medical coders with the knowledge and skills needed for success, while connecting healthcare organizations with top-tier coding professionals. We focus on innovation, skill development, and excellence in healthcare management.
